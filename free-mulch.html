What Is the Free Mulch Program?

When our crews are removing or trimming trees in your area, we chip all the wood debris on-site. Instead of hauling it all to the landfill, we make it available to homeowners, gardeners, and community members who can put it to good use.

Fresh wood chip mulch is one of the best things you can add to your garden. It suppresses weeds, retains soil moisture, regulates soil temperature, and breaks down over time to enrich the soil with organic matter.

What Can I Use It For?

  • Garden beds and vegetable gardens
  • Around trees and shrubs as a moisture barrier
  • Pathways and natural walking surfaces
  • Erosion control on slopes
  • Playground surfacing and play areas
  • Compost pile carbon material

Important: Mulch availability depends on where we're actively working. Call us at 408-858-6123 to check availability and get on the list for your area.
