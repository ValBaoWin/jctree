How much does tree removal cost in San Jose?

Tree removal in San Jose typically costs $300–$600 for small trees, $600–$1,500 for medium trees, and $1,500–$3,000+ for large or hazardous trees. We offer free, no-obligation estimates — call 408-858-6123.

Do I need a permit for tree removal in San Jose?

San Jose requires a permit to remove trees over a certain size on private property. Our ISA Certified Arborist can advise you on whether your tree requires a city permit and help you navigate the process.
